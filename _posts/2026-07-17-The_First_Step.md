date: 2026-07-17

In 2025, after a horrible crisis, I realized that either I manage to get into a new career, or I am not able to live on.

It all began with some steps in a white square.

I started to draw a square and didn't know what to draw into this square. Finally I drew some steps of a stair and then I added a cat's tail and a shoe. I drew another square with a foot and a cat's tail. And another one.

Some weeks later I had created a graphic novel. I thought, it would be great to publish this novel in the internet.

Way back, I had created a website, that I already had forgotten. Now, I thought I could give the page a refresh and put my novel on it.

But when I logged into my account, I realized, that the content creator I used for free before, now wasn't for free anymore. And I was not willing to pay monthly costs for a page, that isn't really as good, as I want it to be. So I provided myself with lots of information about all the different ways to build a website and that's how I first knew about WordPress. 

A software for building Websites, that is free to use, but not so easy to handle, if you don't have any knowledge. I weighed the pros and cons and decided that the best thing would be to learn, instead of taking the easy way and let AI build my website. I invested my time and money in a WordPress course. The best thing I have done in 2025!
