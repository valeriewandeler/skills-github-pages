title: "The First Step"
date: 2026-07-17
---

In 2025, after a horrible crisis, I realized that either I would manage to get into a new career, or I would not be able to live on.

It all began with some steps in a white square.

I started drawing a square and didn't know what to draw inside it. Finally, I drew some steps of a staircase, then added a cat's tail and a shoe. I drew another square with a foot and a cat's tail. And another one.

Some weeks later, I had created a graphic novel. I thought it would be great to publish this novel on the internet.

Way back, I had created a website that I had already forgotten. Now, I thought I could give the page a refresh and put my novel on it.

But when I logged into my account, I realized that the content creator I used for free before wasn't free anymore. And I was not willing to pay monthly costs for a page that isn't really as good as I want it to be. So I gathered a lot of information about the different ways to build a website, and that's how I first learned about WordPress.

Software for building websites that is free to use but not so easy to handle if you don't have any knowledge. I weighed the pros and cons and decided that the best course of action was to learn rather than take the easy way out and let AI build my website. I invested my time and money in a WordPress course. The best thing I have done in 2025!
