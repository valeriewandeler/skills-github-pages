In 2025, after a horrible crisis, I realized that either I manage to get into a new career, or I am not able to live on. In psychiatry, I started to draw and in the end I created a graphic novel. I thought, it would be great to publish this novel in the internet. Way back, I had created a website, that I allready had forgotten. Now, I thought I could give the page a refresh and put my novel on it. But when I logged into my account, I realized, that the content creator I used for free befor, now wasn't for free anymore. And I was not willing to pay monthly costs for a page, that is not really as good, as I want it to be. So I provided myself with lots of information about all the different ways to build a website and tht's how I first knew about WordPress. A software for building Websites, that is free to use, but not so easy to handle, if you don't have any knowledge. I weighed the pros and cons and decided that the best thing is to learn. I invested my money in a WordPress course. The best thing I have done in 2025!
