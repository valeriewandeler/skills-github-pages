title: "A New Level"
date: 2026-07-21
---

In wintertime, as I had become used to doing translations and joining meetings and felt more relaxed with my new occupation, I heard about the idea of starting a learner’s group that was planned to begin in spring. Astonishingly, I was expected to be one of the teachers in this course. I still felt like a WordPress beginner myself and felt quite unsure about this new role.  


Just when I got used to this new challenge, I met an old acquaintance to talk about my professional situation and possibilities to get support for a formation. This person was a cook until forty and then began studying computer science. His story was encouring. He also told me about a coding course, which is for women only and for free. As I was hungry for brain food, I joined this group called Rubymonstas and started to learn Ruby. 


I felt as if I had been transported back to the early days of my college years, when I was cramming for logic. In this group, I felt right at home again, as I did in WordPress. The people I met were all friendly, welcoming, solution-oriented, and empowering. As a person suffering from depression, I need to have such people around me.
