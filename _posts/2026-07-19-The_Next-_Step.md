In July 2025, I joined a summer course "Creating Websites with WordPress". 
We completed the WP Beginner's course in just two weeks and after the course my Website 
<a href="url" target="_blank">https://www.zuezi-bildergeschichte.ch</a> was online. And I was very proud.

The instructor told us a lot about WordPress and shared some insider information with us. He also invited us to contribute to WordPress. 
I took this chance and started to create all the necessary accounts. 
I planned to translate the English content — which I had just used to learn myself — into German. 

Translating wasn't all the work. It was also necessary to create thumbnailsand translate the subtitles in a TTML-Document. By translating I became familiar with a lot of new tools as GitHub, Slack or Figma.

I realized that I had finally found an environment where I feel right at home.
